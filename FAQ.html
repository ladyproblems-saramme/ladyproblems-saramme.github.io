FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What products should I use?

It depends on you! Depending on your lifestyle and prefrences, different products work for different individuals. Take some of our quizzes under the "Quiz" button - we have created these quizzes to help you find what products may be best for you. Our goal is that women do not have to change their activities and lifestyles during their period, but can stay just as confident and active as they usually are.

Who created this website?

We are a group of high school students from the greater Boston area, Massachusettes, and Rhode Island.

What is the point of this website?

We hope to help women of all ages with determining the best products for their health. Based off of not only health patterns, but her personality and lifestyle, we recommend different products and advice!

Why did you create this website?

Our goal is to help women stay confident all the time, no matter if they are on their period or not - we hope women do not change the way they dress, act, or stay active. We were inspired by the P&G Challege to create a reliable, friendly way to engage and help women find out more about their best options for their wellness.
